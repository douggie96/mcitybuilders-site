Bathroom Remodeling in Fitchburg — What the Project Involves

Older Fitchburg homes on Rollstone Hill, in West Fitchburg, and in the Cleghorn neighborhood — built between 1890 and 1950 — typically have small hall baths with original cast-iron tubs, tile surrounds in need of full replacement, and outdated plumbing that requires partial rerouting during any remodel. Lead-based paint is possible in demo layers in pre-1978 construction and must be handled per EPA RRP protocols. Maverick City Builders is RRP-compliant and handles older Fitchburg homes regularly.

Mid-century Cape Cods and ranches in South Fitchburg often have 5x7 hall baths being converted to tile walk-in showers as tubs wear out. This is the single most requested Fitchburg bathroom project we handle — removing the original tub, waterproofing with cement board and RedGard, and building a custom tile shower in the same footprint. These projects require a Fitchburg Building Department permit and run 2 to 3 weeks.

Oak Hill and Route 2A newer colonials typically have larger master baths with builder-grade finishes from the 1990s and 2000s. The most common upgrade is adding a walk-in shower, replacing the single vanity with a double, installing in-floor heat under new tile, and upgrading the lighting. These projects run 3 to 4 weeks and require a Fitchburg Building Department permit for plumbing and electrical work.

Fitchburg Bathroom Remodeling FAQ

How long does a bathroom remodel take in Fitchburg?

A hall bath refresh with tile shower conversion runs 2 to 3 weeks. A full hall bath gut with all-new subfloor, cement board, and tile runs 3 to 4 weeks. A master bath full remodel runs 4 to 5 weeks. We provide a written daily schedule before work begins.

Do you pull permits with the Fitchburg Building Department?

Yes. Bathroom remodels involving plumbing changes, new electrical circuits, or structural modifications require a permit from the Fitchburg Building Department at City Hall on Main Street. Maverick City Builders handles all permit applications, inspections, and sign-offs.

Can you handle lead paint or asbestos in older Fitchburg homes?

Yes. Maverick City Builders is EPA RRP-certified for work in pre-1978 homes. For Fitchburg's older Rollstone Hill and West Fitchburg homes, we follow RRP containment and disposal protocols for any demolition work that may disturb lead-painted surfaces.

Do you convert tubs to walk-in showers in Fitchburg?

Yes — this is our most common Fitchburg bathroom project. We remove the tub, waterproof the subfloor and walls with cement board and RedGard membrane, and build a custom tile shower in the existing footprint. Fitchburg Building Department permit handled by our team.

Do you also work in Leominster, Lancaster, and Clinton?

Yes. Maverick City Builders serves Fitchburg, Leominster, Lancaster, Clinton, Sterling, and surrounding Worcester County towns. Based in Lancaster — about 20 to 25 minutes from Fitchburg — we are fully licensed Massachusetts HIC and CSL.
