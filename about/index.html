    Meet Douglas Mourao — Founder of Maverick City Builders

    Douglas Mourao founded Maverick City Builders to bring a craftsman-first approach to Central Massachusetts homeowners let down by contractors who over-promise and under-deliver. Based in Lancaster, MA. Built on three non-negotiables: show up when promised, communicate proactively, finish what gets started.

    Every project treated as if Douglas's own family were going to live in the home. Shapes material selection, subcontractor vetting, daily cleanup, change orders explained and approved in writing before extra work begins.

    Fully Licensed and Insured in Massachusetts

    Maverick City Builders operates as a fully licensed Massachusetts Home Improvement Contractor (HIC) and Construction Supervisor License (CSL) holder. Carries comprehensive general liability insurance and workers compensation coverage. Pulls every permit required by the local building department — Lancaster, Worcester, and every Worcester County town served — and walks every inspection.

    Where We Build — Worcester County and Central Massachusetts

    Primary service area covers Lancaster, Sterling, Clinton, Bolton, Berlin, Harvard, Hudson, Leominster, Fitchburg, Worcester, Lunenburg, Shirley — Worcester County and Central Mass towns within roughly 25 miles of Lancaster operating base. Full team and tooling brought to each job rather than subcontracting to whoever's cheapest.

    Every project starts with a no-pressure consultation. Douglas or one of our project managers visits the home, walks through the scope, takes measurements, and provides a written estimate within 5-7 business days. No high-pressure sales tactics, no inflated bids designed to leave room for negotiation — just an honest assessment of what the work involves and what it costs. If the scope changes during the project, every change order is documented and approved in writing before any extra work begins, with the cost difference explained line by line.
