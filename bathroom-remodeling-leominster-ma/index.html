Bathroom Remodeling in Leominster — What the Project Involves

Leominster's 1960s–1980s ranches in Orchard Hills and the West Street area typically have dated 5x7 hall baths with tub-shower combos, original cast-iron tubs, and cultured marble surrounds. Most homeowners want tile shower conversions, new vanity and toilet, and updated lighting. These projects require a permit from the Leominster Building Department at City Hall on West Street when plumbing is touched. Typical timeline is 2 to 3 weeks.

South Leominster and Chase Hill area colonials built in the 1990s and 2000s often have undersized master baths with builder-grade finishes. The most common upgrade is adding a walk-in shower, double vanity, and in-floor heat — a project that runs 3 to 4 weeks and requires a Leominster Building Department permit for the plumbing and electrical work involved. We coordinate all inspections and deliver a finished, inspected bathroom.

Leominster's housing stock frequently has unfinished basement space, and adding a new full bath from concrete to tile is a common request. Rough-plumbing and building a new full bath requires a Leominster Building Department permit, floor drain, cement board waterproofing, tile, vanity, toilet, and GFI electrical — a 3 to 4 week project. These basement bath additions significantly increase home value for Leominster multi-family and single-family properties.

Leominster Bathroom Remodeling FAQ

How long does a bathroom remodel take in Leominster, MA?

A hall bath refresh with tile shower conversion runs 2 to 3 weeks. A full hall bath gut (subfloor, cement board, tile, all new fixtures) runs 3 to 4 weeks. A master bath full remodel with walk-in shower, double vanity, and in-floor heat runs 4 to 5 weeks. We provide a written schedule before work begins.

Do you pull permits with the Leominster Building Department?

Yes. Bathroom remodels involving plumbing changes or new electrical circuits require a permit from the Leominster Building Department at Leominster City Hall on West Street. Maverick City Builders handles all permit applications, coordinates inspections, and closes permits as part of every project.

Can you convert a tub to a walk-in shower in Leominster?

Yes. This is our most common Leominster bathroom request. We remove the tub, waterproof the subfloor and walls with cement board and RedGard, and build a custom tile shower in the same footprint — typically 36x60 with a built-in niche and bench. Leominster Building Department permit required and handled by our team.

Do you work in occupied homes in Leominster?

Yes. We coordinate bathroom remodels in occupied Leominster homes with a clear daily schedule — demo in the morning, framing and rough work mid-week, tile and finish in the final days. If you have only one bathroom, we discuss the sequencing before work begins to minimize disruption.
