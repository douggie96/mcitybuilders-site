ABOUT FITCHBURG MA

Fitchburg is a city of about 43,000 in northern Worcester County, bordered by Leominster to the south and Lunenburg to the east. The housing stock is diverse — triple-deckers and multi-family properties in the downtown neighborhoods, post-war capes and colonials in Rollstone and Cleghorn, and larger single-family homes in the Upper Common and Laurel Hill areas. We work throughout Fitchburg on kitchen and bathroom renovations, deck projects, and multi-room remodels.

Permits for Fitchburg projects are filed with the Fitchburg Building Department. Approval timelines run 1-2 weeks for most residential work. We handle all filings and inspections so the project stays on schedule from day one.
