ABOUT LEOMINSTER MA

Leominster is one of the larger cities in northern Worcester County, with a population of about 42,000. The city has a strong mix of triple-deckers and multi-family properties, mid-century single-family homes, and newer subdivisions in areas like Johnny Appleseed and Orchard Hills. We work throughout Leominster on both single-family renovations and multi-unit projects.

Permits in Leominster are filed with the Leominster Inspectional Services Department. For kitchen and bathroom remodels, expect 1-2 weeks for permit approval. We handle all filings and coordinate inspections throughout the project.
