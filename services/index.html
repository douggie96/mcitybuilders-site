    Serving Worcester County and Central Massachusetts

    Maverick City Builders is proud to be the premier local remodeling contractor for homeowners across Central Massachusetts. We actively serve Lancaster, Sterling, Clinton, Bolton, Berlin, Harvard, Hudson, Leominster, Fitchburg, Worcester, Lunenburg, Shirley, and Marlborough. Whether you own a historic New England colonial, a classic mid-century cape, a sprawling suburban ranch, a Victorian-era home, or a multi-family rental property, our experienced team understands the unique structural, plumbing, and design challenges inherent to Worcester County's varied housing stock. From carefully restoring antique architectural details to fully modernizing outdated, closed-off layouts into open-concept living spaces, we bring high-end craftsmanship and reliable, transparent project management to every single build in our community.
