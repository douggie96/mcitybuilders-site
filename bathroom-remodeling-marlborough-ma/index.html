TUB-TO-SHOWER CONVERSIONS IN MARLBOROUGH

The single most requested bathroom upgrade in Marlborough is the tub-to-shower conversion. Marlborough's 1980s and 1990s colonial housing stock — particularly in neighborhoods like French Hill, Hayden Falls, and along Hosmer Street — came standard with fiberglass tub surrounds that now read as dated to every buyer who walks through. Replacing them with a custom tiled walk-in shower is the highest-impact single bathroom improvement in this market.

A tub-to-shower conversion in Marlborough runs $4,500–$9,500 depending on shower footprint, tile selection, and glass door type. A standard alcove conversion with ceramic tile and a sliding door is toward the bottom of that range. A fully tiled walk-in with a custom niche, bench, and frameless glass door is toward the top. The drain relocation — if the existing tub drain isn't in the right position for the shower — adds $800–$1,500 to the project.

All tub-to-shower conversions require a plumbing permit in Marlborough. We file it with the Building Department at 140 Main Street as part of the project. Permit approval in Marlborough typically runs 1–2 weeks for standard bathroom work.

COST BREAKDOWN — MARLBOROUGH BATHROOM COMPONENTS

TILE LABOR (25–35% of budget)

Floor tile install: $8–$15/sqft labor. Shower wall tile: $12–$20/sqft. Custom niche or bench: $400–$900. Tile is the longest phase — budget 1–2 extra days for cure time before grout.

VANITY & FIXTURES (15–25%)

Builder-grade vanity: $300–$800. Mid-range: $800–$2,000. Double vanity: $1,200–$4,000. Faucets and hardware: $200–$800. Popular choice in Marlborough: 60" double vanity for master baths.

PLUMBING (10–20%)

Like-for-like swap: $800–$1,500. Layout change or drain relocation: $2,000–$5,000. Marlborough plumbers: $120–$175/hr. Permit: $300–$600 for plumbing portion.

TILE MATERIALS (8–15%)

Porcelain floor tile: $2–$8/sqft. Wall tile: $3–$15/sqft. Large format 24x24: $5–$18/sqft. Mosaic accent tile: $12–$30/sqft. Order 10% overage for cuts.

ELECTRICAL & LIGHTING

GFCI outlets, exhaust fan, vanity lighting: $600–$1,400. Heated floor element and thermostat: $800–$1,800 installed. Required for all new bathrooms under current Marlborough code.

PERMITS (MARLBOROUGH)

Building + plumbing permit: $400–$900. Filed at 140 Main Street, Marlborough Building Dept. Approval: 1–2 weeks standard, 2–3 weeks if electrical changes are involved.
