DECK BUILDING IN LANCASTER MA

Lancaster is our home base. We've built decks on properties throughout Lancaster — from smaller capes on Side Hill Road to larger colonials along Route 117 and on wooded lots off Lunenburg Road. Lancaster's mix of older homes with original small decks or no deck at all is a steady source of deck projects every spring and summer.

All deck permits in Lancaster are filed with the Lancaster Building Department at Town Hall. Most residential deck permits are approved within 1-2 weeks. We handle all permit applications and coordinate the required footing inspections before and during construction.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much does a deck cost in Lancaster MA?

Deck costs in Lancaster MA range from $8,000 for a small pressure-treated deck to $32,000+ for a large composite deck with stairs, railings, and built-in features. A typical 16x16 composite deck runs $16,000-$24,000 installed in 2025.

Do I need a permit for a deck in Lancaster MA?

Yes. Any attached deck requires a building permit from the Lancaster Building Department. Freestanding decks over 200 sq ft also require permits. We file all permit applications and coordinate inspections.

How long does it take to build a deck in Lancaster?

A standard 16x16 deck takes 4-7 days of active construction. Add 1-2 weeks for Lancaster Building Department permit approval before work starts. Total timeline from signed contract: 2-4 weeks.

What deck brands do you install in Lancaster?

We install Trex, TimberTech, Fiberon, and Azek composite decking, as well as traditional pressure-treated lumber. We'll recommend the right product for your budget, maintenance preference, and project scope.
