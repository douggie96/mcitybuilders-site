COST BY SCOPE LEVEL

Basic Refresh

$18,000–$28,000

Repaint existing cabinets, new hardware, new countertops (laminate or entry-level quartz), new faucet and sink, new flooring. Layout stays the same. No electrical or plumbing moves.

Mid-Range Remodel

$28,000–$48,000

New semi-custom cabinets, quartz countertops, tile backsplash, updated lighting, new appliances, new flooring. Minor layout adjustments. This is the most common scope for Worcester County homes.

Full Gut Renovation

$48,000–$75,000+

Complete demo to studs. New custom or high-end semi-custom cabinets, premium countertops (quartz or stone), reconfigured layout, new electrical panel circuits, plumbing relocation, island addition, high-end appliances.

WHAT DRIVES THE COST

CABINETS (30–40% of budget)

Stock: $3k–$8k · Semi-custom: $8k–$18k · Custom: $18k–$35k+

COUNTERTOPS (10–15% of budget)

Laminate: $1.5k–$3k · Quartz: $3k–$8k · Stone: $5k–$14k

LABOR (20–35% of budget)

Worcester County labor: $75–$130/hr. Full kitchen labor: $8k–$20k depending on scope.

APPLIANCES (10–20% of budget)

Builder-grade suite: $3k–$6k · Mid-range: $6k–$12k · High-end: $12k–$25k+

FLOORING (5–10% of budget)

LVP: $2k–$4k · Hardwood: $4k–$9k · Tile: $3k–$7k installed.

PERMITS & FEES

Worcester permits: $500–$1,500 for a full kitchen remodel. Timeline: 1–2 weeks for approval.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does a kitchen remodel take in Worcester County?

4–8 weeks for a mid-range remodel. Demo: 2–3 days. Rough work (electrical/plumbing): 1 week. Cabinet install: 3–5 days. Countertop template and fabrication: 1–2 weeks. Finish work: 1 week. Permit approvals in Worcester add 1–2 weeks upfront.

What is the ROI on a kitchen remodel in Massachusetts?

Worcester County homeowners typically recoup 65–72% of mid-range kitchen remodel costs at resale. A $35,000 remodel adds roughly $22,000–$25,000 in appraised value. The bigger return is marketability — updated kitchens sell faster and reduce days on market significantly.

Should I get multiple quotes for a kitchen remodel?

Yes — get 3 written quotes minimum. Compare scope carefully, not just price. A quote $8,000 lower may exclude permit fees, haul-away, or finishing work that another quote includes. Verify each contractor's HIC and CSL license before signing anything.
