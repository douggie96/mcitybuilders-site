COST BREAKDOWN BY COMPONENT

TILE LABOR (25–35% of budget)

Floor tile install: $8–$15/sqft labor. Shower wall tile: $12–$20/sqft labor. Custom niche or bench adds $400–$900.

VANITY & FIXTURES (15–25%)

Builder-grade vanity: $300–$800. Mid-range: $800–$2,000. Double vanity: $1,200–$4,000. Faucets/hardware: $200–$800.

PLUMBING (10–20%)

Like-for-like plumbing swap: $800–$1,500. Layout change (moving drain/supply): $2,000–$5,000. Worcester plumbers: $120–$180/hr.

TILE MATERIALS (8–15%)

Porcelain floor tile: $2–$8/sqft material. Wall tile: $3–$15/sqft. Large format (24x24): $5–$18/sqft. Mosaic accents: $12–$30/sqft.

ELECTRICAL & LIGHTING

GFCI outlets, exhaust fan, vanity lighting: $600–$1,400. Heated floor element + thermostat: $800–$1,800 installed.

PERMITS (Worcester)

Building + plumbing permit: $400–$900. Permit approval time: 1–2 weeks. Required for any plumbing or electrical work.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does a bathroom remodel take in Worcester County?

Standard hall bath: 2–3 weeks. Master bath gut renovation: 3–5 weeks. Factor in 1–2 weeks upfront for permit approval in Worcester before demo starts. Tile work is the longest phase — large format tile takes longer to set and requires cure time before grout.

Do I need a permit for a bathroom remodel in Massachusetts?

Yes, if any plumbing or electrical work is involved — which is almost every bathroom remodel. A licensed contractor pulls the permit. In Worcester, permits are filed with Inspectional Services. Unpermitted plumbing work is a red flag for buyers and can void insurance coverage.

What's the ROI on a bathroom remodel in Massachusetts?

Mid-range bathroom remodels in Massachusetts return 60–70% of cost at resale on average. A $16,000 remodel adds roughly $10,000–$11,000 in appraised value. Master bath additions return more in higher-value markets. Updated bathrooms also reduce days on market significantly.

Tub-to-shower conversion — what does it cost in Worcester County?

A tub-to-shower conversion in Worcester County runs $4,500–$9,500 depending on shower size, tile selection, and whether the drain needs to be relocated. A custom tiled walk-in shower with built-in niche and frameless glass door is toward the top of that range. An alcove shower with prefab walls is toward the bottom.
