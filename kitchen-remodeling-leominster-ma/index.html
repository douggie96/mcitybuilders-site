How long does a kitchen remodel take in Leominster?

Do you pull permits for Leominster kitchen remodels?

Can I stay home during the kitchen remodel?

Yes. We set up a temporary kitchen area, use dust barriers between work zones, and schedule noisiest work for daytime hours. Most disruption is in the first 3-5 days of demo.
